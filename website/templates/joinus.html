Becoming a Chapter Lead

The Sunshine Squad's chapter leads are a crucial part of our organization, responsible for organizing events and building community in their local area. As a chapter lead, you'll have the opportunity to make a real difference in your community by bringing seniors and teens together through fun and engaging activities. We provide resources and support to help you get started, and we're always here to answer any questions you may have along the way. Whether you're interested in hosting a craft night, organizing a game day, or coming up with your own unique event idea, we encourage you to join our team and help us spread sunshine in your community!

The Importance of Chapters

The Sunshine Squad runs on a chapter-based model, where volunteers can start their own local chapter and organize events in their community. This allows us to expand our reach and impact, while also giving volunteers the opportunity to take ownership of their involvement and make a difference in their own community. We provide resources and support to help volunteers get started, and we encourage creativity and initiative in organizing events that align with our mission of building connection between seniors and teens.

Why Join Us?

As a smaller nonprofit, The Sunshine Squad offers a unique opportunity to make a real difference in your community. By joining our team, you'll be part of a passionate group dedicated to building connections between seniors and teens. You'll gain valuable experience in leadership, event planning, and community engagement while contributing to a cause that matters.
