Our Bi-Monthly Newsletter

The Sunshine Squad's bi-monthly newsletter details all that The Sunshine Squad has done within that period of time, including highlights from our nursing home visits, Instagram updates, and upcoming volunteer opportunities. We also include a "Volunteer Spotlight" section, where we feature one of our volunteers and their experience with The Sunshine Squad. To subscribe to our newsletter, please email us at sunshinesquadco@gmail.com. To check out old issues, take a look below!

Want to Make Our Newsletters?

The Sunshine Squad is always looking for volunteers to help us create our bi-monthly newsletter! If you're interested in writing, graphic design, or just want to help out, please email us at sunshinesquadco@gmail.com., or DM us on Instagram @sunshinesquadco. We would love to have you on our team!
