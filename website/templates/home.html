Future Speakers Initiative

With Future Speakers Initiative, we're empowering the next generation of civic leaders through accessible speech and debate education. We provide every child with the opportunity to grow as an advocate and learn the communication skills they'll need to know for life, completely free of cost.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower middle school students with essential communication, critical thinking, and public speaking skills through accessible speech and debate resources and training. We are committed to advocating for educational equity by bringing high-quality speech and debate programs to communities that lack access in their school curriculum. We envision a future where every student, regardless of background, has the tools to speak up, lead, and drive change in their communities.
