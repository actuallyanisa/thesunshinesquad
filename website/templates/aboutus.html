Our Mission

At Future Speakers Initiative, we believe that communication is power, and every student deserves the tools to use it. In a world where opportunities are often shaped by access and privilege, strong speaking and argumentation skills should not be reserved for the few. Founded by Yashnil Mohanty, FSI was created to bridge the gap in speech and debate education by offering accessible, high-quality programs that don’t just teach students how to speak, but how to lead. What sets us apart is our focus on advocacy: empowering middle schoolers to find their voice, speak out on the issues they care about, and become changemakers in their communities. We don’t just build confident speakers. We aim to nurture the next generation of civic leaders.

Our Story

Competing for Westmont High School, we saw firsthand how disparities in funding and resources impact students’ ability to compete on equal footing. Many middle schoolers in underfunded schools never get the chance to experience debate, practice public speaking, or sharpen their critical thinking, skills that are crucial for academic success, career advancement, and self-advocacy. This inequality creates a cycle where students from wealthier backgrounds become more articulate and confident, while students from disadvantaged communities struggle to find their voice.

Our Awards

Future Speakers Initiative has been honored to receive recognition for our work in expanding access to speech and debate education. We're honored to have our work acknowledged officially by Campbell's Mayor, Sergio Lopez, and San Jose's Mayor, Matt Mahan. Additionally, we've had our work recognized by California Senator Dave Cortese, who has commended our efforts to empower students through communication skills. These recognitions reflect our commitment to creating opportunities for all students to find their voice and succeed in speech and debate.

Our Classes

Future Speakers Initiative offers free, high-quality classes in public speaking, debate, and advocacy for middle school students. Our curriculum is designed to be engaging, interactive, and accessible to students of all backgrounds and skill levels. We focus on building foundational skills in argumentation, delivery, and critical thinking while also empowering students to speak out on issues they care about. Our classes are taught by experienced coaches and mentors who are passionate about helping students find their voice and become confident communicators. Whether your child is new to speech and debate or looking to sharpen their skills, our classes provide a supportive environment for growth and learning.
