The Debate Hour

The Debate Hour features spontaneous, unfiltered debates on current-event topics and popular NSDA PF, Policy, and LD resolutions, all designed to make argumentation more approachable for students. Hosted by Yashnil Mohanty and Jake Oderio, each episode breaks down real-time thinking, structured reasoning, and how anyone can learn to speak with confidence.

The Future Speakers Initiative's Youtube Channel

The FSI YouTube Channel offers free, high-quality lessons and playlists covering speech fundamentals, debate techniques, argumentation, delivery, confidence-building, and competitive event strategy. These videos are designed for middle and high school students, especially those without access to formal coaching or structured programs. Whether you're just getting started or sharpening advanced skills, our channel helps you grow as a communicator.
